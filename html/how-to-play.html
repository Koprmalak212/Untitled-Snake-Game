Objective

  • ▶
    Eat the glowing food pellets scattered across the arena to grow longer and earn points.
  • ▶
    The longer your snake, the higher your score — and the harder it is to survive.
  • ▶
    Outlast all other players in the arena to claim the top spot on the leaderboard.
⬤

Food & Scoring

Food Effect Points
Standard Pellet Grow +1 segment +10 PTS
Rare Pellet Grow +3 segments +50 PTS
Remnant Left by a dead snake — worth eating fast +5 PTS EA.

* Food respawns at a random location after being eaten.

💀

Death & Respawn

01

Your snake dies if it hits a wall, runs into its own body, or collides with another player's snake.

02

A Game Over screen appears showing your final score, length, and arena rank.

03

Wait out the short respawn cooldown, then hit Play Again to re-enter at a random spawn point.

04

Your body segments become collectible remnants for other players to eat.

💡

Tips

Stay near the edge early

The arena center is chaotic. Hug the walls while you're small and farm pellets safely.

Cut off opponents

Curl around a longer snake to block their path. They'll crash into you — and you'll collect all their remnants.

Watch the mini-map

The mini-map in the corner shows nearby threats. Check it often so you don't get surprised.

Your name helps

Your name floats above your head. A memorable name makes you a target — pick wisely.
